Tactical Precision

Designed for both beginners and seasoned operators. Every detail is tuned for accurate timing and effective learning.

Advanced Keying

Full support for Iambic A, Iambic B, and Straight Key modes. Includes adjustable polarity and strict timing checks.

Low Latency Audio

Real-time oscillator synthesis using the Web Audio API. Choose between pure sine or crisp sawtooth waves for maximum readability.

Adaptive Learning

The system identifies characters you struggle with and increases their frequency in random groups and word training.

Detailed Analytics

Visualize your progress with WPM history charts. Track error rates per character and session duration.

Farnsworth Timing

Bridge the gap between learning and speed. Maintain high character speeds while extending spacing to give your brain time to process.

Mobile Optimized

Train anywhere. Responsive touch paddles utilize your device's multi-touch capabilities for accurate keying on the go.

Scientific Learning Method

Morse Pro moves beyond rote memorization. We use visual mnemonics and rhythmic phrases to create lasting mental associations.

  • Visual Hints Overlays dots and dashes directly onto characters to bridge visual and auditory memory.
  • Q-Codes & Abbreviations Dedicated modules for common radio prosigns and Q-codes (QTH, QSL, 73).
  • RX & TX Training Don't just listen. Practice sending with instant feedback on your timing and rhythm.
